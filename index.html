Research

Undergraduate Thesis

Domain-Adaptive Cross-Lingual Transfer for Low-Resource Bangla Medical Named Entity Recognition

Islamic University of Technology (IUT) · 2025 – Present

Bangla has almost no labeled medical text to train language models on — a gap that leaves Bangla-speaking populations underserved by the healthcare AI tools being built for high-resource languages. My thesis addresses this directly: adapting multilingual transformer models, through transfer learning and careful dataset construction, to recognize diseases, drugs, and symptoms inside Bangla clinical text.

The work sits at the intersection of natural language processing, low-resource machine learning, and applied healthcare informatics — combining dataset preparation, model fine-tuning, and rigorous evaluation using Python-based research workflows.

Field

Method

Domain
